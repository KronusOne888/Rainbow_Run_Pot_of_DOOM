RAINBOW RUN

Pot of Doom

FOLLOW THE RAINBOW • TAKE THE GOLD • OUTRUN THE FOREST
Mobile-first • One-file HTML • Three.js • Single Player
Threat Tier 0 ready

Follow the rainbow through a cursed forest, kill the laser-eyed leprechaun guarding the pot of gold, steal the treasure, and escape to the edge of the map before the whole forest tears you apart.

Deer, squires, bears, and bobcats look innocent from a distance. Get too close and they become ravenous. If the woodland creatures capture you, your run ends on a spit over a ritual fire while they perform a seance around you.

Controls

Touch Controls

  • Move with the left joystick
  • Look by dragging the right side
  • FIRE bottom-right
  • JUMP, SWITCH, and GRENADE on the right

Desktop Shooter Controls

  • W A S D move and strafe
  • MOUSE look
  • LMB fire
  • SPACE jump
  • Q or Wheel switch weapon
  • 1-8 direct weapon select
  • G throw grenade

Start with a pistol and shotgun. Find the super shotgun, nailgun, machine gun, grenade launcher, rocket launcher, flamethrower, plus grenades, health, armor, and ammo. Grab the gold, then sprint to the forest edge to win.
